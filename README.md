# My AI/ML Blog

Learning AI by building real projects.

## About Me

I'm an AI/ML learner and builder writing about LLMs, training pipelines, and the messy debugging that happens between idea and deployment. No fluff — just notes from the terminal.

My focus is on **practical AI development**: understanding how modern language models work, implementing them from scratch, debugging training pipelines, and shipping working systems to production.

## What I Write About

My blog is organized into four categories:

- **Tutorials** — Step-by-step guides building real AI systems
- **Debugging Fixes** — Errors I hit, root causes, and the fix
- **Deployment Guides** — Shipping models to production without pain
- **AI Experiments** — Half-broken ideas that taught me something


## Get Involved

- **Read the blog** — Explore tutorials, experiments, and debugging deep dives
- **Check out projects** — See the code behind what I've built
- **Follow along** — Latest notes on AI, ML, and building real systems
